What We Do

We are Top Quality Miniature Painters and are in the Business of Painting Mini's for those war-gammers that are too harried in life to paint their own, or for those that feel they lack the ability to paint. We don't judge why you are using us, we all know wargaming is better with painted minis. Who likes to play on a field of grey minis.


We paint your minis to the level that you can afford so you feel proud to charge across the wind swept fields on your way to total victory.

We all love to play, and nothing looks better than having a nicely painted army on the board while kicking butt… and on those rare occasions… receiving a nice butt kicking Please check out our gallery and prices by clicking the links!
