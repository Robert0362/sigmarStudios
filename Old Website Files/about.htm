My name is Robert and I have been painting High Quality minatures for well over 6 years.

I started playing Warhammer in 2001 and as soon as I started Playing I knew that Painting and building the models was the part that captivated me. My first Army was nowhere near what I would call acceptable nowdays, but everyonbe startes somewhere. I currently have 3 Warhammer Fantasy armies and 2 Warhammer 40K armies. I have a love of playing the good guys, (I guess it is a Hero Complex) but I am planning a Chaos Fantasy army.

Why Should you use Sigmar Studios?
